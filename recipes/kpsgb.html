King Prawn and Scallop in Ginger Butter

Description

Prawns and scallops in a ginger butter sauce with shredded carrot and leeks. Delicious served with plain basmati rice.

Ingredients

  • ⅓ cup white wine
  • 1 teaspoon chopped fresh ginger
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 small carrot, chopped
  • ½ leek, chopped
  • 10 sea scallops, halved
  • ⅓ pound tiger prawns, peeled and deveined
  • ½ cup unsalted butter, cubed
  • chopped fresh chives for garnish
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Bring the wine, vegetable stock, ginger, and garlic to boil in a skillet.
  2. Mix in the carrot and leek, and reduce heat to low.
  3. Simmer 5 minutes.
  4. Mix in scallops and prawns.
  5. Stir in the butter cubes.
  6. Continue cooking until butter is melted and scallops and prawns are opaque.
  7. Sprinkle with chives, and season with salt and pepper to serve.
